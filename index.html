About Me

SarcJae is a filipino freelance artist that prioritizes fun and whimsy with video game and animation media to the forefront of all their works.


Specializing in digital illustration and media alike, most of Jae’s works are of the digital variety.


Cartoony, animated, and stylized art are the main styles of Jae’s works.
